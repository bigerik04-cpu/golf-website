Golf Basics

This website is about golf: the basics, practice tips, and my favorite clubs.

What is Golf?

Golf is a sport where you hit a ball into a series of holes using clubs. The goal is to finish each hole in the fewest strokes.

A typical round is 18 holes, and players use different clubs for different distances and situations.

Basic Equipment

These are the main items you’ll see golfers using on the course.

Must-have items (Unordered List)

  • Golf clubs (driver, irons, wedges, putter)
  • Golf balls
  • Tees
  • Golf glove
  • Bag and towel

Beginner Practice Plan

If you’re new, focus on the basics first: solid contact, short game, and putting.

Steps to improve (Ordered List)

  1. Warm up with short putts (3–5 feet).
  2. Hit chips and pitches to learn touch.
  3. Practice irons for consistent contact.
  4. Finish with driver swings (control first, power later).
