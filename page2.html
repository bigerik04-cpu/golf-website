More Golf Tips

This page goes deeper into golf tips, common mistakes, and what I focus on when I practice.

Common Beginner Mistakes

A lot of new golfers swing too hard and lose control. A smooth swing usually works better than trying to crush it.

Another mistake is ignoring the short game. Chipping and putting can save the most strokes.

My Practice Focus

When I practice, I try to build consistency first, then speed.

What I work on most

  • Putting speed control (long putts)
  • Chipping contact (clean strikes)
  • Fairway shots with irons/hybrids
